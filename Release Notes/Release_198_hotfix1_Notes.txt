MSFS Hangar Release 198 Hotfix 1

Fixes:
- Fixed Aircraft Detail Research tab crash caused by undefined aircraftSiteLinks reference.
- Research tab now rebuilds aircraft and airport site shortcuts locally so the tab no longer depends on outer-scope variables.
- Reduced Google/captcha regression by avoiding automatic search-provider page loads when opening the Research browser in desktop mode.
- Research browser now opens to a blank pane initially and uses Bing for the default fallback search path in the Research tab.
- Saved-mode browser fallback also opens a blank pane instead of triggering empty-provider searches.
