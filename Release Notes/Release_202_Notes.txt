MSFS Hangar Release 202

Flight polish and telemetry refinement:
- Range estimate now uses live fuel remaining and fuel flow instead of the old placeholder formula.
- Added fuel remaining display in gallons and percent.
- Improved breadcrumb retention and flight trail visibility.
- Reworked flight marker silhouettes with larger black aircraft icons.
- Improved continuous aircraft motion by starting new interpolation from the current drawn pose.
- Increased Flight Map height and reduced lower card padding so the map has more room.
- Renamed the follow control to Center aircraft and kept Center now as a one-shot action.
