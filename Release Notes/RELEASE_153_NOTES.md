MSFS Hangar Release 153

Focused on Explore Globe controls, behavior, and readability.

Changes:
- Do not show feet in the distance-from-ground readout once above 1 mile from the ground.
- Marker click now selects the airport and flies to the lighter "Zoom In" airport view instead of jumping to max zoom.
- Globe controls cleaned up: + and - buttons, heading/compass with degree readout, moved distance readout, removed overlapping built-in zoom controls.
- Added Center On/Off behavior for selection-following while allowing the user to pan off the airport.
- Distance/measure tool now behaves as a two-point measure tool with visible points and a clear action.
- Explore Globe defaults to Bright styling and keeps airport marker colors from the airport legend.
- Current-airport Center remains a refocus action without forcing a deep zoom.
- Updated saved globe defaults to a real top-level globe-style start view.
